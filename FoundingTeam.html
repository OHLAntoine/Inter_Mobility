Lukas Neckermann (Chief Executive Officer, Neckermann Strategic Advisor)

For over a decade, Lukas has helped companies and investors navigate the #mobilityrevolution. He is a leading strategist, advisor, researcher, and keynote speaker on Smart Cities and Smart Mobility.
After introducing key industries to “The Mobility Revolution"​ with his 2014 book, Lukas followed up with three more: "Corporate Mobility Breakthrough 2020", "Smart Cities, Smart Mobility", and "Being Driven". He has addressed over 200 audiences on electrification, autonomous vehicles, infrastructure, energy, shared mobility, and investing into a carbon-neutral future.
Lukas is an Advisor to NEXT Modular Vehicles, MSCI, SNRG and other companies, as well as a GLG Council Member. He regularly lectures at New York University, TU Berlin, the University of St. Gallen, as well as corporate universities across Europe. He is part of multiple startup event juries and a member of the AutoTech Europe 2023 Advisory Board. Lukas was COO at mobility scale-up Splyt, helping to grow the company to over 100 staff post-Series B investment, and has also co-founded companies in the UK, US, and Romania. He recently co-initiated and launched PAVE Europe, a not-for-profit organization to educate on the benefits of automated vehicle technology.
After starting his career in marketing and strategy at BMW, Lukas subsequently advanced through to several Director/Board appointments at Allianz Group companies in Germany, the UK, and Ireland, before venturing into entrepreneurship.
Founding Intermobility was a good way to bring his knowledge and extensive network to companies all over Europe.
